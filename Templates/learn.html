Welcome to FinDice

Life can feel like a game of chance when it comes to managing money. Every expense, every swipe of the card — it’s like rolling the dice and hoping for the best. But with FinDice, we’re here to put the odds in your favor. Our platform helps you take control of your finances, making every move a strategic one.

Our Mission

Managing multiple accounts, tracking expenses, and staying within budget can often feel overwhelming. FinDice simplifies this process by automating transaction categorization, analyzing spending habits, and offering actionable financial insights — all in one intuitive platform. Whether you're looking to track income and expenses or make smarter financial decisions, FinDice has the tools you need to succeed.

Features of FinDice

FinDice is designed to provide users with both basic and advanced financial management tools. Our core features include:

  • Transaction categorization with bank integration through the Plaid API.
  • Real-time alerts to notify users when they are nearing their budget limits.
  • Visual reports and financial trend analysis using the Google Charts API.
  • AI-driven insights for premium users, offering personalized advice and predictive budget suggestions.

FinDice helps users stay informed and in control of their finances, offering both regular and premium services to meet diverse needs.

Getting Started

Getting started with FinDice is easy! Simply create an account, link your bank accounts using the Plaid API, and start tracking your expenses. For premium users, we offer advanced features such as AI-driven financial advice and custom budget predictions. With FinDice, you can make smarter financial decisions every step of the way.

Why Choose FinDice?

Unlike other financial management tools, FinDice goes beyond basic tracking. It offers predictive insights and actionable advice tailored to your unique financial habits. With a seamless bank integration and an intuitive interface, FinDice is designed to help users of all financial literacy levels take control of their financial future.
